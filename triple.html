Factorized Diffusion
[Triple Hybrids Gallery]

We can make triple hybrid images by conditioning three different levels of a Laplacian pyramid on different prompts. We found that this was fairly difficult to do, and required manually hand tuning the Laplacian pyramid parameters. If you have difficulty seeing the prompts please try zooming in and out, or stepping a couple meters away from the screen.
